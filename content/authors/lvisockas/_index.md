I started my PhD in February 2026 at the University of West London, collaborating with the University of Glasgow and UKHSA. My research focuses on applying Machine Learning to predict Influenza A reassortment events and host spillover risks. I am interested in how ML models can be used on biological data to study viral epidemiology, and how it can be used to prepare for zoonotic epidemics and pandemics.
